Learn Crystal Healing–A Practitioner’s Certification Course

Learn how to work with crystals for energy healing, chakra balance, emotional release, and protection.
Cleanse. Program. Heal. Transform.

Crystals are ancient energy tools that carry the healing vibrations of the Earth. When used intentionally and ethically, they can help us restore balance, clear negativity, and support the healing of others.
In this course, you will learn not just the theory of crystals, but how to choose and work with them energetically using Reiki and intention-setting. This is a hands-on, intuitive training designed for healers, spiritual seekers, and wellness professionals


What You Will Learn

How to Cleanse Crystals

Crystals absorb energy from their environment and need regular cleansing before and after use. You'll learn multiple methods, including:

  • Soaking crystals in salt water, incense smoke
  • Reiki / Lama Fera based energetic cleansing

How to Program Crystals with Intention

Programming a crystal gives it a specific purpose for healing. You will learn:

  • How to hold and connect with the crystal
  • Using meditation and breath to set intention
  • Speaking the intention or silently infusing it into the crystal
  • Sealing the programming with Reiki or Lama Fera

How to Choose the Right Crystal for Your Client

You will learn how to use Reiki or chakra scanning to understand what the client needs:

  • Scan the chakras to find blocked or weak areas
  • Identify emotional, mental, or energetic imbalances
  • Match the right crystal(s) based on the chakra’s condition and client’s concern
  • Decide whether to give a single crystal or a custom combination

Combining Crystals with Reiki or Lama Fera

You’ll also learn how to combine crystal therapy with healing systems:

Reiki / Lama Fera

  • Place programmed crystals on or around chakras during a Reiki session
  • Charge the crystals with Reiki/ Lama Fera symbols
  • Use crystals to amplify healing intention during self-healing or client work

This synergy enhances both the healing and protective power of your sessions.
