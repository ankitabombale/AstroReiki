What is Nadi Astrology?

$125/session

Nadi Astrology is one of the most advanced and accurate systems of Indian astrology.It provides highly specific, life -event predictions by analysing your birth chart through a three-layered approach— Sign, Nakshatra(Constellation), and Sub-Lord.
According to the principles of Nadi Astrology the Zodiac is divided into 12 equal sub divisions of Signs with equal subdivisions of the 27 Nakshatras.These Nakshatras are further divided into unequal segments called Sub-Lords. Nadi Astrology gives prime importance to the Sub-Lord, which carries more predictive weight than the Nakshatra, and in turn, more than the planet itself.


Why Nadi Astrology is Unique

Unlike traditional systems such as Parashari or Jaimini astrology, Nadi Astrology emphasizes pinpoint accuracy by judging every planet on three platforms

• Sign
• Nakshatara
• Sub-Lord

It uses your date, time, and place of birthto prepare the Lagna Chart and Nirayana Bhava Chalit Chart, providing a highly refined and personalized analysis.
Nadi Astrology considers not just individual houses but combinations of houses to predict life events. For timing, it uses the Vimshottari Dasha system along with planetary transits, making it the only truly predictive Hindu astrology system
